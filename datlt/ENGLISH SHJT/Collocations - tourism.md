|Collocation|Example|Meaning / Comment|
|---|---|---|
|uninterrupted view|From most rooms there are uninterrupted views of the castle.|Nothing blocks the view.|
|panoramic view|From the top floor restaurant diners have a panoramic view of the countryside.|View over a very wide area.|
|spectacular view|From the balcony there is a spectacular view of the mountains.|Very dramatic and impressive view.|
|enjoy / admire a view|We stopped for a few minutes to admire the view.|Look at a view with pleasure.|
|breathtaking scenery|The area has some breathtaking scenery – mountains, cliffs, lakes.|Extremely striking and beautiful views.|
|dominate the landscape|The castle dominates the landscape for miles around.|Can be seen from far away.|
|beach stretches|A beautiful beach stretches for miles along the coast.|Beach continues for a long distance.|
|sandy beach|The hotel has its own sandy beach.|Beach with sand (not rocks).|
|secluded beach|You’ll love the secluded beaches.|Quiet, not many people.|
|golden sands|You can wander for miles along the golden sands.|Sand that looks golden in colour.|
|peaceful / tranquil countryside|You can quickly go from the town to the tranquil countryside.|Calm and quiet area (formal).|
